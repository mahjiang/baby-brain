## Key Questions to Ask

- [ ] What are the visitor/support person policies?
- [ ] Where do we park / enter for labor?
- [ ] What does the room look like? Private or shared?
- [ ] What is the C-section rate?
- [ ] Is there a NICU on site? What level?
- [ ] What is the nurse-to-patient ratio?
- [ ] Lactation consultant availability?
- [ ] What do they provide (diapers, pads, pump, etc.)?
- [ ] How long is a typical stay (vaginal vs C-section)?
- [ ] What is the policy on eating during labor?
- [ ] Is there wireless monitoring available?
- [ ] What are the pre-registration steps?

## Pre-Registration

- [ ] Pre-register at hospital (usually available online, do by week 32-34)
- [ ] Confirm insurance coverage and pre-authorization if needed
