| Class | Priority | When to Take | Signed Up? | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------------|------------|-------|
| **Childbirth / labor prep** | HIGH | Weeks 24-32 | [ ] | Covers stages of labor, coping, when to go to hospital |
| **Infant CPR / first aid** | HIGH | Weeks 28-36 | [ ] | Often offered by hospitals, Red Cross, or local fire dept |

| **Newborn care basics** | Medium | Weeks 30-36 | [ ] | Bathing, diapering, soothing, what's normal |
