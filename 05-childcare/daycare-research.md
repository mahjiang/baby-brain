## What to Ask on Tours

### Safety & Licensing
- [ ] Licensed by state? Inspection results available?
- [ ] Staff-to-child ratios (infant rooms)?
- [ ] Background checks for all staff?
- [ ] What is the sick child policy?
- [ ] How are emergencies handled?

### Daily Life
- [ ] What does a typical day look like for infants?
- [ ] How are naps handled? (safe sleep practices?)
- [ ] How is feeding handled? (breast milk, formula, solids later)
- [ ] Diapering routine and communication?
- [ ] Outdoor time?

### Communication & Philosophy
- [ ] Daily reports (app, paper, verbal)?
- [ ] How do they handle crying / soothing?
- [ ] What is the educational philosophy?
- [ ] Staff turnover rate?
- [ ] How do they handle developmental concerns?

### Logistics
- [ ] Hours of operation?
- [ ] Closure days / holidays?
- [ ] Late pickup policy and fees?
- [ ] Payment schedule and method?
- [ ] Waitlist process and deposit?
- [ ] Transition / adjustment period for new babies?
