# Backup / Emergency Care

> Regardless of your primary childcare choice, you need backup plans.

## When You Need Backup Care

- Baby is sick (most daycares won't take sick kids)
- Nanny is sick or on vacation
- Daycare closure days
- Parent emergencies

## Options to Research

- [ ] Does either employer offer backup care benefits? (Bright Horizons backup care is common)
- [ ] Family members available on short notice?
- [ ] Drop-in daycare options nearby?
- [ ] Trusted babysitter list (build this early)
- [ ] Care.com or UrbanSitter for last-minute sitters
