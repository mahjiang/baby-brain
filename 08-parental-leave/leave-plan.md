## Stagger Strategy

> Consider staggering leave to maximize total time with baby before childcare starts.

**Option A:** Both take leave simultaneously
- Pro: Both parents home together
- Con: Shorter total coverage

**Option B:** Stagger (one starts, other starts when first returns)
- Pro: Longer total time before childcare needed
- Con: One parent solo for a period

**Option C:** Overlap first 2-4 weeks, then stagger
- Pro: Both home during hardest adjustment period, then extended coverage
- Con: Requires coordination with employers

- [ ] Discuss stagger preference with partner
- [ ] Confirm flexibility with both employers
