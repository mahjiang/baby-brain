## Benefits & Tax Advantages

- [ ] **Dependent Care FSA** — Up to $5,000/year pre-tax for childcare. Must elect during open enrollment or within 30 days of birth (qualifying life event).
- [ ] **Health Insurance** — Add baby within 30 days of birth. Compare plans if open enrollment is near.
- [ ] **Child Tax Credit** — Up to $2,000/child (2024; check current year)
- [ ] **State programs** — Check state-specific benefits (paid family leave, childcare subsidies)
- [ ] **Life insurance** — Consider or increase coverage after baby
- [ ] **Will / estate planning** — Designate guardianship
