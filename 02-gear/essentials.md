## What You Do NOT Need

- Wipe warmer
- Bottle warmer (a cup of warm water works)
- Baby shoes (until they walk)
- Matching crib bedding sets (bumpers, blankets, pillows — these are **unsafe**, per AAP)
- Stuffed animals in the crib (**unsafe** for sleep)
- Baby food processor (regular blender/fork works)
- Too many newborn-size clothes (they outgrow fast)
