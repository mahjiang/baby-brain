# Pediatrician Search

> **Research by:** Week 24-28 | **Decide by:** Week 34 | Baby needs a pediatrician visit within 3-5 days of birth.

## What to Look For

- Board-certified pediatrician (or family medicine doctor)
- Accepts your insurance
- Convenient location (you'll go a LOT in year one)
- Office hours and after-hours availability
- Hospital affiliation (ideally same as delivery hospital)
- Appointment availability (same-day sick visits?)

## Interview Questions

- [ ] What is your approach to vaccines? (Looking for AAP-aligned schedule)
- [ ] How do you handle after-hours calls/emergencies?
- [ ] Same-day sick visits available?
- [ ] What is the typical wait time for well-visits?
- [ ] Do you have lactation support or can you refer?
- [ ] How do you communicate with parents? (Portal, phone, email?)
- [ ] What hospital are you affiliated with?
