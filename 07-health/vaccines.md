# Vaccine Schedule

> Follow the AAP/CDC recommended schedule. Source: [CDC Immunization Schedule](https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/schedules/hcp/imz/child-adolescent.html)

## Recommended Schedule (First Year)

| Age | Vaccines | Notes |
|-----|----------|-------|
| Birth | Hepatitis B (dose 1) | Given at hospital |
| 2 months | DTaP, IPV, Hib, PCV15, RV, Hep B (dose 2) | First big appointment; multiple shots |
| 4 months | DTaP, IPV, Hib, PCV15, RV | |
| 6 months | DTaP, Hib, PCV15, Hep B (dose 3), RV (dose 3 if Rotateq), Flu (if flu season) | |
| 9 months | Flu (dose 2, if first flu season) | |
| 12 months | MMR, Varicella, Hep A, PCV15 (booster) | |

## What to Expect

- Fussiness, mild fever, and soreness at injection site are normal
- Infant Tylenol (acetaminophen) can help — dose by weight, ask pediatrician
- No Ibuprofen until 6 months
- Multiple vaccines at once is safe and well-studied
